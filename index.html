About Me

My name is Mengting Gu (古梦婷). I'm a Staff Research Scientist at Visa Research. Prior to joining Visa, I obtained my M.S. in Computer Science and Ph.D. in Computational Biology & Bioinformatics from Yale University. My theis is advised by Dr. Mark Gerstein, on large scale learning algorithms for genomics functional annotation.

My research areas include Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing and Computational Biology.
